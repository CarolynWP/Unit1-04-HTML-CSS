An Intro to my Favourite TV Series.

The Umbrella Academy is a 2-season series on Netflix. It was created for TV by Steve Blackman, but was heavily based off the popular comic book series "The Umbrella Academy" by Gerard Way and Gabriel Ba.

The Plot

On October 1st, 1989, 43 superpowered children around the world were born from women who were not pregnant when the day first started.
An eccentric billionaire, Sir Reginald Hargreeves, takes it upon himself to adopt as many of these children as possible. He ended up with only 7, and raises these children in the "Umbrella Academy", training them to fight crime.
Years later, long after they grew up and left their home, they reunite again after news of Sir Reginald's death and find out they have ten days to save the world from an unknown apocalypse.
